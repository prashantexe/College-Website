Vision

  1. To reform entrepreneurial student aspirants into start-up establishment
  2. To support & render start-up into a profitable entity.
  3. To transform innovation into scalable business models with high productive impact and also encouraging interdisciplinary advancement.
  4. To create impact for economic development and community wealth.

Mission

  1. To recognize and nurture students, faculty members and alumni into early stage startups
  2. To create and promote collaborative networks through partnerships between academia, industry and government.
  3. To increase and advance the in-house operational skills for leveraging entrepreneurial expertise and resource network.
  4. To identify & nurture top-notch entrepreneurs expert mentoring and also to foster lean startups to sustainable companies.

Value

  • Integrity
  • Culture of innovation
  • Sustainability
  • Triple 'M' Strategy (Mentor, Money and Market)
